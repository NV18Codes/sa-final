S.A. Electricals: Trusted Electrical Contractors Since 1980, Powering Performance and Reliability

Since 1980, S.A. Electricals has earned a reputation as a trusted electrical contractor in the industry. As a Class 1 government-licensed electrical contractor and engineer, we provide complete solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From electrical installation to complex power distribution systems, our expertise ensures safe, reliable, and efficient results every time.

With over four decades of experience, we focus on delivering quality workmanship, completing projects on time, and providing long-term value for our clients.

Comprehensive Electrical Services

At S.A. Electricals, we offer a wide range of specialized electrical services designed to meet modern infrastructure needs:

  • Electrical Installation

    We handle everything from wiring new buildings to setting up advanced electrical systems. Our electrical installation services are performed with precision, safety, and compliance with CEIG approvals.

  • Electrical Maintenance

    Regular maintenance is crucial for preventing unexpected breakdowns. Our team ensures your systems run smoothly, which reduces the risks of downtime and costly repairs.

  • Lighting Design and Installation

    Proper lighting improves safety, aesthetics, and efficiency. We provide lighting design and installation for homes, offices, factories, and outdoor spaces.

  • Power Distribution

    We specialize in designing and installing strong power distribution systems. This includes high tension and low tension systems, substations, and custom panel boards to ensure uninterrupted performance.

  • Electrical Troubleshooting

    Our skilled technicians offer quick and accurate troubleshooting to find faults, restore power, and keep disruptions to a minimum.

  • Generator Installation

    To protect against power outages, we provide professional generator installation and integration to ensure a continuous power supply for essential operations.

  • Electrical Panel Upgrades

    Outdated systems can compromise safety and efficiency. Our panel upgrades modernize your infrastructure, improving load management and system reliability.

  • Cable Management

    Well-organized wiring reduces hazards and boosts efficiency. Our cable management solutions provide safe, structured, and durable installations.

  • Earthing and Lightning Protection

    Protecting people and equipment is our priority. We offer reliable earthing and lightning protection systems to minimize the risks of electrical surges and ensure compliance with safety standards.

  • Energy Efficiency Solutions

    We help businesses and homeowners cut costs and reduce their environmental impact with energy-efficient solutions. Our systems optimize power usage.
