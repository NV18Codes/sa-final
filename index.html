ELECTRICAL CONTRACTORS & ENGINEER

As a Class 1 government-licensed contractor with a legacy dating back to 1980, S.A. Electricals is your single, trusted partner for comprehensive turnkey MEPF solutions. We bring this expertise to every residential, commercial, and industrial project, managing the entire lifecycle from initial design and CEIG approvals to the expert installation of complex High-Tension (HT) & Low-Tension (LT) systems, sub-stations, custom panel boards, and integrated plumbing and fire safety infrastructure. Our holistic, single-source approach guarantees a single point of accountability, ensuring your project is delivered with unparalleled safety, quality, and on-time completion while simplifying coordination and reducing total project cost from start to finish
