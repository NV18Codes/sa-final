S.A. ELECTRICAL
ELECTRICAL CONTRACTORS & ENGINEER

As a Class 1 government-licensed electrical contractor with a history dating back to 1980, S.A. Electricals is your only trustworthy partner for full turnkey MEPF solutions. We apply this expertise to every residential, commercial, and industrial project, from initial design and CEIG approvals to expert electrical installation, trustworthy plumbing services, and certified fire safety equipment suppliers. Our work includes complex HT and LT systems, sub-stations, custom panel boards, integrated home plumbing services, tap plumbing, and fire fighting equipment. With a plumber on call, access to local plumbers, and our professional fire safety company's proven fire extinguisher safety procedures, our all-inclusive, single-source approach guarantees safety, quality, and and on-time completion while simplifying coordination and reducing total project cost from start to finish.
