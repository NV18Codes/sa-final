S.A. Electricals: Reliable Plumbing Services for Every Need

Since 1980, S.A. Electricals has been a trusted name in providing complete engineering solutions. Alongside our skills in electrical contracting, we offer reliable plumbing services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From pipe installation to water treatment solutions, our dedicated plumbing team ensures efficiency, safety, and dependability in every project.

With skilled local plumbers available, and decades of experience, we simplify plumbing tasks with a professional, single-source approach that saves both time and money.

Comprehensive Plumbing Services

Our plumbing team provides a wide range of solutions designed to keep systems running smoothly and prevent costly problems:

  • Pipe Installation

    Durable, high-quality pipe installation is the backbone of any plumbing system. We use the best materials and proven methods to ensure leak-free performance and long-lasting reliability.

  • Leak Detection and Repair

    Even small leaks can cause significant water damage. Our leak detection and repair services quickly identify problems, minimizing disruption and saving money.

  • Drainage System Installation

    Effective drainage system installation prevents flooding and protects properties. We design and install drainage systems for both residential and large-scale projects.

  • Water Heater Installation

    From residential heaters to commercial water heating systems, our installation services ensure safe setup and consistent performance.

  • Plumbing Maintenance

    Regular maintenance keeps systems running efficiently and prevents breakdowns. Our expert plumbers provide timely checks and repairs to ensure everything functions properly.

  • Bathroom and Kitchen Fitting

    We specialize in bathroom and kitchen fittings, including taps, sinks, and complete plumbing system integration. Our services combine usability with modern design.

  • Water Treatment Solutions

    Clean and safe water is essential. Our treatment solutions provide filtration and purification systems tailored to your needs.

  • Gas Fitting

    Safety is essential in gas fitting. Our licensed plumbers install and maintain gas pipelines in strict accordance with safety codes and standards.

  • Sewerage System Installation

    We handle sewerage system installation expertly, ensuring proper waste management, compliance, and long-lasting durability.

  • Plumbing System Design

    Every project starts with careful planning. Our plumbing system design ensures efficient layouts, cost savings, and adherence to building standards.
