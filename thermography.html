Electrical Services

Power Quality Analysis

Identifying and diagnosing power quality issues like voltage sags, swells, and harmonics to ensure efficient and reliable operation.

Thermography

Using infrared imaging to detect overheating components in electrical systems, preventing potential failures and fire hazards.

Battery Impedance Testing

Assessing the health and reliability of backup power and UPS batteries by measuring their internal impedance to predict failure.

Earth Pit Testing

Measuring the resistance of an earthing system to ensure it provides a safe path for fault currents, protecting personnel and equipment.

Socket Testing

Verifying that electrical outlets are wired correctly and safely, checking for correct polarity, earthing, and identifying potential faults.
