S.A. Electricals – Specialized Testing and Analysis Services for Safer Systems

At S.A. Electricals, genuine reliability is more than just installation—constant surveillance, testing, and preventive maintenance are needed. Since 1980, as a Class 1 government-accredited electrical contractor, we've been providing sophisticated electrical testing services to boost safety, efficiency, and system performance. Our expert services include power quality analysis, thermography, battery impedance testing, earth pit testing, and socket testing, all aimed at catching problems before they develop into expensive breakdowns.

We believe in proactive measures to ensure your electrical systems are not just operational, but optimally safe and efficient. Our comprehensive approach helps you avoid costly downtime and extend the lifespan of your critical infrastructure.

Our Specialized Testing and Analysis Services:

  • Power Quality Analysis

    Erratic voltage or poor power quality destroys sensitive devices and raises energy expenses. Our power quality analysis services assist in the identification of fluctuations, harmonics, and inefficiencies to provide stable and reliable energy distribution.

  • Thermography

    With advanced infrared imaging technology, our thermography services identify overheated components, loose connections, or concealed faults. The non-invasive technique facilitates preventive maintenance, eliminating the risk of electrical fires or system breakdowns.

  • Battery Impedance Testing

    Backup systems are only as good as their batteries. Our battery impedance testing checks the health and performance of batteries, keeping critical systems such as UPS and emergency power in working condition when most needed.

  • Earth Pit Testing

    Safeguarding with the right grounding is critical. Through earth pit testing, we take measurements and test the efficiency of earthing systems to shield people and equipment from electrical shock and surges.

  • Socket Testing

    Faulty sockets may lead to disruptions, inefficiencies, or danger. Our socket testing services guarantee that connections are safe, compliant, and able to support operational loads.
